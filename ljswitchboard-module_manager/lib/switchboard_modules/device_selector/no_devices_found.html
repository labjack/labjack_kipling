Kipling did not find any devices. Double check your scanning options (USB, Ethernet, WiFi), make sure the device is powered on, and press the "refresh devices" button to try again. If your device is still not found, try connecting it to your computer via USB.

If your device is reporting a yellow or grey Ethernet or WiFi connection button then you are experiencing connection issues. Please go to our website for device setup and troubleshooting tips:

  • Setup Ethernet & WiFi
  • Basic Networking & Troubleshooting
  • Kipling Selecting Devices
  • Ethernet Settings

If you are connected over USB and still can't find your device, look at the device's LEDs to make sure they are blinking properly. You may need to re-set your device using the SPC screw terminal.

  • T7 LEDs
  • T7 SPC Terminal

Kipling supports the following LabJack devices:

  • T7/T7-Pro
  • T4

Kipling does not support the following LabJack devices:

  • U6/U6-Pro
  • U3-HV/U3-LV
  • UE9/UE9-Pro
  • U12
  • Digit-TL/Digit-TLH

Navigate to LabJack's LabJack Applications website page to determine the correct application for your device.
